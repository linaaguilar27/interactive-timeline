Public Consultations on PCI Revision

FSC launches consultations on the revision of its Principles and Criteria, International Generic Indicators, and other forest management normative documents (collectively referred to as PCI).

The consultation is open from 01 October 2025 until 30 November 2025.

Consultation is available in English, Spanish, and French on the FSC Consultation Platform. Please see the Consultation Content, Consultation Overview, and the Consultation Guideline for further details.

Upcoming Webinars

To facilitate participation in the public consultation, webinars will be held in English with simultaneous translation into Spanish and French. These webinars will provide an overview of the consultation process and address questions and comments from participants.
