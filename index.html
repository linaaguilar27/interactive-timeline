2026 – Drafting Phase Begins

  • January: Analysis of consultation results and Working Group (WG) applicants.
  • February: Secretariat updates the Draft ToR and shares the consultation synopsis, revised ToR, and list of WG applicants for approval.
  • March: BM 105
    • Discussion of updated ToR and WG composition
    • Approval of ToR
  • April: First WG meeting (online) to establish house rules.
  • May: First in-person WG meeting. Focus on main topics, starting with Outcome Orientation.
  • June–September: Virtual WG meetings. Secretariat conducts virtual meetings and runs consultative forums.
  • November: Second in-person WG meeting. Review outstanding topics and prioritize key issues for agreement.
  • December: WG-approved Draft 1-01 PCI. Secretariat initiates work on the second public consultation.
